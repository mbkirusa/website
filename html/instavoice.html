Frequently Asked Questions

What is InstaVoice?

InstaVoice is a cloud-based call completion service that offers visual voicemail, missed call alerts, and reachability notifications.

Can I access InstaVoice when my phone is off?

Yes, InstaVoice stores your voicemails and alerts in the cloud, so you can access them anytime, even if your phone is off.

Does InstaVoice support voicemail transcription?

Yes, InstaVoice provides visual voicemail with transcription, making it easy to read and manage messages.

How does the reachability notification work?

InstaVoice notifies you when a contact becomes reachable, ensuring you can connect at the right time.
