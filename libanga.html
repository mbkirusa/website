Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request a celebrity video on Libanga?

Visit our website, choose a celebrity, specify the wish details and delivery date, then complete the secure payment at checkout. The video is delivered via WhatsApp or SMS on the special day.

You’re refunded and can request another star with a discount, backed by our 100% fulfillment guarantee.

Can I personalize the video with a name?

Yes, include the recipient’s name, the sender’s name, and the type of wish when placing your request on our website.

How do I share the video with others?

On the scheduled date, both you and the recipient receive the video via WhatsApp or SMS, ready to share and celebrate.
