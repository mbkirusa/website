Your Libanga Experience

Secure Login

Engaging Feeds

Explore infinite-scroll feeds, like, comment, and repost. Search users or celebrities, view trending content, and create rich blog posts with text, images, audio, or video.

Personalized Profile

View and edit your profile, including posts, followers, and following lists. Update your name, bio, and profile picture to showcase your journey.

Celebrity Connections

Book a Celebrity Greeting in 4 Easy Steps

1. Choose a Celebrity

Search and select from a diverse roster of global stars. View their profiles, blogs, and availability for bookings.

2. Select a Slot

Pick a convenient slot and delivery timing based on the celebrity’s lead time availability. Confirm your selection before proceeding.

3. Confirm & Pay

Review your booking details, including the message, recipient, and delivery platform. Complete the payment securely to schedule your greeting.

4. Receive Your Greeting

Once the celebrity uploads the greeting, receive your personalized video. Share it with loved ones or on social media to celebrate the moment!
