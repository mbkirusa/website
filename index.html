About Kirusa

Our Evolution

Established in 2001 as a trailblazer in voice and messaging solutions, Kirusa has evolved into a leading AI innovator by 2025. Originating from AT&T Bell Labs, we now empower over 100 million users across 50 countries.

This rich heritage drives our advanced AI solutions, including Corpusio, KirusaAssist, Libanga, and hAIppy Fit, revolutionizing productivity, connectivity, and personal wellness on a global scale.

Our Vision

Kirusa is dedicated to harnessing artificial intelligence to enhance lives worldwide. We deliver intelligent, user-focused solutions that unlock critical knowledge, foster meaningful connections, and promote well-being.

Operating in 18 countries, our commitment to trust, innovation, and excellence shapes a future where technology seamlessly elevates human potential.

Life at Kirusa

At Kirusa, we thrive in a dynamic, global tech-first environment that fosters innovation and creativity. Our diverse, close-knit team balances fun and hard work, creating a space where learning never stops. Guided by seasoned experts with numerous patents and accolades, Kirusa is a place where your ideas can flourish.

Our Leaders

Dr. Inderpal Singh Mumick

Founder, Chairman, and CEO of Kirusa, Dr. Inderpal Singh Mumick is a visionary entrepreneur with over 20 years in telecom innovation. Under his leadership, Kirusa has pioneered transformative technologies like Voice SMS and InstaVoice, impacting over 100 million monthly users across Africa, Asia, and Latin America. Previously, he co-founded Savera Systems, leading it as CEO and CTO to innovate web-based billing solutions, and worked at AT&T Laboratories on pioneering billing projects. Dr. Mumick holds 32 issued patents, with 10 pending, and has authored 40 technical papers in leading journals.

Dr. Raja N. Moorthy

Chief Operating Officer at Kirusa, Dr. Raja N. Moorthy brings over 40 years of telecom expertise. With 18 years at Kirusa, he has overseen India operations, technical management, and R&D, previously serving as Vice President of Engineering in NJ, USA. He has led innovative software development, including at Savera Inc. and Zyglobe, and established NIT Trichy’s OCTAGON computer center in the 1990s. Dr. Moorthy, also a Senior Consultant to Gupshup and Adjunct Professor at Dayanand Sagar University, holds a Ph.D. from IIT Delhi and 5 patents in AI, IoT, and mobile applications.

Baldeep Dua

Chief Financial Officer at Kirusa, Baldeep Dua drives financial strategy with over 20 years of expertise. She oversees capital raising, mergers and acquisitions, strategic planning, profitability enhancement, and private placements, ensuring Kirusa’s financial growth and stability with her extensive experience across diverse roles.
