About Kirusa

Our Journey

Founded in 2001 by technocrats from AT&T Bell Labs, Kirusa has evolved from a pioneer in voice and messaging to a leader in AI innovation. Headquartered in New Providence, NJ, with offices in Africa, India, and Europe, we serve over 100 million monthly users across 50 countries, partnering with 50+ mobile carriers.

Our legacy in mobile social media and enterprise messaging laid the foundation for our AI-driven future, addressing enterprise productivity, personal connections, and wellness with solutions like Corpusio, KirusaAssist, Libanga, and hAIppy Fit.

Our Mission

At Kirusa, we leverage artificial intelligence and connectivity to enrich every aspect of life. Our mission is to deliver powerful, user-focused solutions that empower businesses to unlock knowledge, help individuals connect in meaningful ways, and coach people to healthier lives.

With a global presence in 18 countries and a commitment to trust and innovation, Kirusa is redefining how technology enhances human experiences.
