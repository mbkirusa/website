Corpusio: AI-Powered Enterprise Knowledge Hub

Unlock instant access to your enterprise's documents, images, audio, and video with cutting-edge AI technology. Corpusio reduces search time by up to 80%, delivering precise, actionable answers tailored to your role. With robust role-based access control (RBAC), it ensures sensitive information is securely restricted to authorized users only. Seamlessly accessible via WhatsApp and our intuitive website, starting at just $150/month, Corpusio transforms the way your team works: smarter, faster, and more connected.

KirusaAssist: Your AI-Powered Virtual Companion

Transform your meetings with KirusaAssist, an AI-powered virtual assistant that captures insights, coordinates across teams, and delivers instant, role-based answers. It joins your calls, takes real-time notes, syncs updates across simultaneous meetings, and integrates seamlessly with Corpusio for secure knowledge access. Ask KirusaAssist a question during meetings, and it provides answers via text-to-speech, reading them aloud, while displaying the response in the chat, restricted to your authorized role for a fluid, hands-free, and private experience.
