Key Features & Benefits

Personalized Fitness Journey

hAIppy Fit’s AI Coach builds your fitness and wellness profile by asking about your age, gender, height, current and target weight, fitness goals, activity level. In just minutes, it creates a tailored workout and wellness plan—and updates it weekly based on your progress and feedback to keep your journey effective, flexible, and engaging.

Work Out Anywhere, Anytime

hAIppy Fit uses AI to recommend a personalized workout plan tailored to you, with step-by-step videos and voice guidance to support your fitness journey wherever you are—no gym or equipment required.

Real-Time Guidance & Feedback

Follow on-screen instructions while you exercise. Our intelligent AI monitors your form, counts reps, and distinguishes between correct and incorrect movements to help you improve every day. No video content is saved or shared. Only your data points are captured to help improve your workout experience.

Summary & Insights

At the end of each session, receive a detailed summary of your performance—highlighting what you did well and where you can improve. Progress is just a tap away.

Weekly Personalized Nutrition Plans

A healthy, balanced diet looks different for everyone—hAIppy Fit suggests Weekly Meal Plans every week tailored to your diet type, cuisine preferences, medical conditions, allergies, and favorite foods to create nutrition plans that truly work for you.
