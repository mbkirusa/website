Make Meetings Smarter

Meetings should spark ideas, not bog you down with note-taking. KirusaAssist is an AI-powered virtual assistant that joins your calls, captures real-time notes, and syncs updates across simultaneous meetings. Integrated with Corpusio, it delivers instant, role-restricted answers from your enterprise knowledge. Ask KirusaAssist a question during meetings—via text or audio—and it provides answers through text and audio, while displaying the response in the chat, ensuring only authorized data is accessed through a secure authentication process. Transform meetings into productive, secure, and insight-driven sessions.

Key Features & Benefits

Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)

Restricts meeting insights and Corpusio answers to users’ assigned roles, verified via secure authentication. Ensures privacy by limiting access to authorized information, securely storing all data.

Real-Time Summarization

Transcribes meetings with high accuracy and generates summaries with decisions, action items, and quotes. Customizable for formal minutes or casual notes, respecting role-based restrictions.

Cross-Meeting Coordination

Joins multiple meetings, syncing updates and ensuring team alignment. Shares insights limited to authorized users, reducing follow-up time.

Context-Aware Q&A

Answers queries during meetings (e.g., “What’s our Q3 revenue?”) using Corpusio’s knowledge hub, delivering precise, cited responses in chat and via text-to-speech, restricted to role-based permissions.

Automated Follow-Ups

Extracts action items (e.g., “Send report by Friday”) and drafts emails with summaries and tasks, ensuring accountability for authorized users.

Seamless Integration

Joins Zoom, Teams, Google Meet, and Webex as an AI participant. Integrates with Slack, Salesforce, and Corpusio for a unified, secure workflow.

Intelligent Search & Insights

Search past meetings (e.g., “Project Orion deadline”) and analyze participation or sentiment, with access restricted to authorized roles, transforming meetings into a secure knowledge base.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q).How does KirusaAssist ensure privacy during meetings?

KirusaAssist uses Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) with secure authentication to restrict meeting insights and answers to authorized roles, ensuring data privacy.

Q).Does KirusaAssist work with Zoom and Microsoft Teams?

Yes, it joins Zoom, Teams, Google Meet, and Webex as an AI participant, posting notes and answers in chat or reading them aloud via text-to-speech, restricted to authorized users.

Q).How does KirusaAssist handle questions during meetings?

Ask a question, and KirusaAssist queries Corpusio’s authorized data, displaying answers in chat and reading them aloud via text-to-speech, ensuring role-based privacy.

Q).Is there a free version of KirusaAssist?

Yes, the Starter plan offers 3 meetings/month with basic transcription and summaries, secured by role-based access. Pro plans add unlimited meetings and analytics.

Q).What is the authentication process for accessing meeting data?

Users undergo a secure authentication process to verify their role, ensuring only authorized individuals can access meeting insights and Corpusio answers.
