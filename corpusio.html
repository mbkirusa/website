Tired of Document Overload?

Enterprises lose countless hours daily searching through scattered documents—reports, contracts, emails, audio, and video. Manual digging leads to missed insights and delayed decisions.

Corpusio changes that. Powered by Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and Llama 2, Corpusio ingests your documents in any format, understands context, and delivers precise answers instantly. It’s your enterprise’s super-smart librarian, eliminating silos and empowering your team with knowledge on demand.

Key Features & Benefits

Ask Anything, Get Answers

Pose questions in plain English (or 10+ languages). Corpusio reads through your files and delivers concise, cited answers—like “What were our Q3 revenue growth drivers?”—with references to exact reports, saving countless hours.

Unified Search Across Silos

Connects to repositories like SharePoint, Google Drive, and Box, building a unified index of your knowledge. Respects security permissions, ensuring users only see authorized content—your single source of truth.

Multimodal Document Processing

Analyzes text, images, audio, and video (on higher plans). Auto-summarizes reports, extracts key metrics (e.g., financial figures, deadlines), and flags inconsistencies, acting like an AI analyst 24/7.

Developer-Friendly APIs

Offers APIs and integrations to pipe answers into Slack, BI tools, or intranet widgets. Developers can build custom apps, like a chatbot for employee questions, powered by Corpusio’s brain.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Corpusio work with SharePoint and Google Drive?

Yes—Corpusio integrates with major document repositories like SharePoint, Google Drive, Box, and databases. It builds a unified index while respecting your security permissions, ensuring seamless access to authorized content.

Can Corpusio process audio and video files?

Yes, on Pro and Enterprise plans, Corpusio supports multimodal understanding, analyzing text, images, audio, and video files to extract insights and answer questions across formats.

How does Corpusio ensure answer accuracy compared to Vectra?

Like Vectra, Corpusio uses a hybrid of Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and FAISS vector search to minimize AI hallucinations, ensuring factually correct answers with cited references. Our multimodal support and quick deployment give us an edge.

Is there a free trial for Corpusio?

Yes, we offer a free pilot program. Contact us to let Corpusio loose on your documents and witness the insights emerge—no commitment required.
