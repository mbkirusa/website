Frequently Asked Questions

How does Voice SMS work?

Record a voice message, and Voice SMS converts it into an SMS link that recipients can access to listen to your message.

Is Voice SMS available in multiple languages?

Yes, Voice SMS supports voice messages in any language, making it ideal for multilingual regions.

Can businesses use Voice SMS?

Absolutely, businesses can use Voice SMS for promotional campaigns, customer updates, or transactional messages.

How do recipients access the voice message?

Recipients receive an SMS with a link to the voice message, which they can play directly on their device.
